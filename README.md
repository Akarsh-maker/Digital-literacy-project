## 📌 Project Overview
As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have developed this Digital Literacy Portfolio to serve as a resource for my peers. This repository documents my journey through five key modules, ranging from visual communication to cybercrime prevention. 

Every task included here is designed to foster a safer, more professional, and highly collaborative digital environment for our batch.

---

## 📂 Module Summaries & Deliverables

### 🎨 [Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness](./task-1-presentation/)
* **Focus:** Creating visual resources to explain Digital Literacy.
* **Deliverable:** An infographic titled "The Digital Student Blueprint" covering core definitions, tools, and cyber hygiene.

### 👤 [Task 2: Professional Digital Portfolio](./task-2-portfolio/)
* **Focus:** Establishing a professional online presence.
* **Deliverable:** Screenshots and links to my GitHub Profile README, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow accounts.

### 💻 [Task 3: Coding & Collaboration](./task-3-platforms/)
* **Focus:** Technical skill-building and data collection.
* **Deliverable:** Proof of HackerRank challenge completion and a 5-question 'Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz' created via Google Forms.

### ✉️ [Task 4: Professional Communication](./task-4-email-etiquette/)
* **Focus:** Mastering formal email tone and social media ethics.
* **Deliverable:** Formal email drafts for extension requests and internships, plus a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist.

### 🛡️ [Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness](./task-5-cybercrime/)
* **Focus:** Recognizing and preventing online threats in India.
* **Deliverable:** A case study on UPI Payment Fraud and a comprehensive 'Stay Safe Online' prevention guide.
